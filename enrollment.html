The Registration Process

1

Research

Determine your child's educational needs and objectives. Ask, "what does he or she need to succeed?" It is the responsibility of all of us - parents, teachers, community members, and students - to work together to help every child succeed.

2

Get to Know Us

Visit our schools to meet with our director, principals and staff. Understand our programs and how AAA Academy can serve your child's specific needs. Review the student handbook to understand the rules and regulation.

3

Register

Fill out the student registration packet and submit registration requirements to us.

Things To Know First

The registration packet is required for students registering to any AAA Academy school.
